About me

Welcome to my website!

My name is Tyee Fellows. I am Family Medicine Resident who has a passion to care for others, an unquenchable thirst for knowledge, and a life mission to advocate for equity for the communities that I am a proud member of - Medical, Indigenous (Inuit), and Disability (Dyslexia).

The motivation for this website is to familiarise myself with HTML, Javascript and CSS languages. These languages are ubiquitous in our modern life and offer a lot of potential for innovation, especially with Web3 around the corner. I hope to translate some of these skills into the world of medicine to ultimately improve patient care.

In the spirit of open-source data, the code to all parts of this website have been made public at my GitHub for any avid learners like myself. If you have any question(s), suggestion(s) and/or find find any error(s) in my source code, please contact me directly at my personal email.

Thank you for visiting. I hope you enjoy the website!
